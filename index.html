About

I am a MEXT 2025 Scholar pursuing a Master of Economics at Kobe University's Graduate School of International Cooperation Studies (GSICS), studying digital literacy, education economics, and development policy in the ASEAN region under Professor Keiichi Ogawa.

With an MBA from Heriot-Watt University and over seven years leading international educational partnerships across Myanmar, Thailand, the UK, and the USA, I bring a rare combination of field-tested management experience and rigorous academic inquiry.

A recent internship at the World Bank (Republic of Congo, 2026) and active membership in the Japan Comparative Education Society reinforce my commitment to evidence-based development policy. I am eager to contribute to international organisations and institutions at the intersection of education, technology, and sustainable development.
